Requirements

  • Flexible different resolution, different processes for different needs, links to other models, across sectors and across scales (nested approach)
  • Adjustable to be tailored to the needs at IIASA i.e. collaboration with other programs/models, including solutions and option as part of the model
  • Agent based to be able to incorporate the stakeholders options
  • Comparable to keep the link to ISI-MIP for multi-model approaches (model comparison)
  • Modular to choose between different approaches, linkage to other models in various ways
  • Well documented Including documentation, automatic source code documentation (Wiki - Doxygen)
  • Multi-disciplinary e.g. including the social science perspectives (Socio-hydrology e.g. Sivapalan et al. 2012)
  • Multi-platform to be used on different platforms (PC, clusters, super-computers)
  • Community driven lead by IIASA but open-source
